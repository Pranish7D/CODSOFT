Our Classes

Vinyasa Yoga

Vinyasa Yoga is a dynamic form of yoga that connects breath with movement. It's often characterized by flowing sequences and smooth transitions between poses, making it a great choice for building strength, flexibility, and mindfulness.

Benefits of Vinyasa Yoga

  • Improves cardiovascular health
  • Increases flexibility and strength
  • Enhances focus and concentration

Hatha Yoga

Hatha Yoga is a traditional form of yoga that focuses on physical postures (asanas) and breath control (pranayama). It's great for beginners and practitioners of all levels, offering a gentle yet effective way to improve flexibility, balance, and relaxation.

Benefits of Hatha Yoga

  • Promotes relaxation and stress relief
  • Increases flexibility and balance
  • Improves posture and core strength

Yin Yoga

Yin Yoga is a slow-paced style of yoga that focuses on deep stretching and relaxation. It involves holding passive poses for longer periods, typically 3 to 5 minutes, to target the connective tissues and improve flexibility and joint mobility.

Benefits of Yin Yoga

  • Targets deep connective tissues
  • Increases flexibility and joint mobility
  • Calms the mind and reduces stress

Restorative Yoga

Restorative Yoga is a gentle and therapeutic practice that focuses on relaxation and rejuvenation. It involves using props such as blankets, bolsters, and blocks to support the body in passive poses, allowing for deep rest and stress relief.

Benefits of Restorative Yoga

  • Promotes deep relaxation and stress relief
  • Supports healing and recovery
  • Enhances body awareness and mindfulness

Benefits of Yoga

  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Improves flexibility and strength
  • Increases mindfulness and focus
  • Promotes better sleep
